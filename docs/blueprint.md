# **App Name**: 北海学園大学新聞会

## Core Features:

- Latest Articles Display: Showcases the most recent news articles on the homepage in an easy-to-read format.
- Category-Based Article Browsing: Allows users to filter and browse articles by various categories such as campus news, events, interviews, etc.
- Full Article View: Provides a dedicated page for each article, displaying its full content, images, and publishing details.
- Admin Content Management: A secure, authenticated dashboard for newspaper club members to create, edit, and publish new articles.
- Rich Text Article Editor: An intuitive editor within the admin panel for formatting article text, adding images, and managing categories.
- AI Article Summary Tool: Automatically generates a concise summary for newly created or updated articles to provide quick insights for readers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Light scheme for readability and a clean aesthetic suitable for news.
- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#336699) representing trustworthiness and academic rigor.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#E8EDF2) to ensure focus on content while maintaining brand consistency.
- Accent color: A soft, muted cyan (#85CCCC) to provide a refreshing highlight and complement the primary blue.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable design across all content types.
- Utilize simple, line-based icons for navigation, article actions, and administrative functions, ensuring clarity and a contemporary feel.
- A responsive grid-based layout for article listings, prioritizing easy navigation and prominent display of featured news across devices. Individual articles will use a clean, single-column layout for optimal reading.
- Subtle, non-distracting fade and slide-in animations for content loading and state changes to enhance user experience without hindering speed.
